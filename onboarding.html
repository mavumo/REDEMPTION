Which service are you interested in?

California Record Expungement

We specialize in navigating the complexities of felony expungements to clear your record under Penal Code 1203.4 (within California) for just $990.00. Let us handle the paperwork, court filings, and advocate on your behalf so you can move forward with a clean slate.

Certificate of Rehabilitation

If you served time in state prison and cannot expunge your conviction, we can petition the court under PC 4852.01 for a Certificate of Rehabilitation—an official endorsement that your past is behind you and you’re a contributing member of society.

Arrest Record Sealing

Arrest record sealing conceals your records from public view, making past arrests inaccessible in routine checks. We assess your case, prepare paperwork, and represent you in any hearings.

Early Probation Termination

Under PC 1203.3, you can ask the court to end probation early. Many clients then immediately file for expungement—streamlining the path to a clean record.
