Certificate of Rehabilitation

If you have a criminal conviction and served time in state prison in California, unfortunately the underlying conviction cannot be expunged. However, we can assist you in petitioning the court under California Penal Code 4852.01 for a Certificate of Rehabilitation. This certificate is a powerful endorsement from the court, indicating that your criminal history is in the past and that you’re a valued, contributing member of society.

Arrest Record Sealing

Similar to expungement, arrest record sealing conceals your arrest records from the public, making your past arrests inaccessible through routine background checks. Sometimes, even individuals who were never convicted but were arrested may face discrimination. Our process includes a detailed case assessment, completion of paperwork, and representation in any necessary hearings.

Early Probation Termination

Under California Penal Code 1203.3, individuals on probation can petition the court to terminate probation early. Our most satisfied clients are those who obtain early termination and then immediately file for expungement—streamlining the process and maximizing your chances of a clean record.
