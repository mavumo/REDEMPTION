Frequently asked questions
Who qualifies for arrest record sealing?
Generally, individuals who were arrested but not convicted (charges dismissed or never filed) are eligible. Each case is reviewed to determine eligibility.
Does sealing remove the record completely?
Sealing removes your arrest record from public view and most background checks, but law enforcement agencies and courts may still have access.
How long does the process take?
The process can take anywhere from 90 to 180 days, depending on the jurisdiction and case complexity.
