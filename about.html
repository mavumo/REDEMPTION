Empowering Californians with a Clean Slate


Redemption Law Group is a California-based law firm committed to helping individuals reclaim their futures by clearing criminal records and restoring dignity.

We specialize in Criminal Record Expungement, Arrest Record Sealing, Certificates of Rehabilitation, and Early Probation Termination.

Our mission is simple but powerful: to give people a second chance at a better life.

Our Story
Founded with a heart for justice, Redemption Law Group was born from the belief that past mistakes shouldn't determine someone's future. Every day, we help people in California who’ve served their time or fulfilled probation take the legal steps needed to leave their past behind. Whether it’s through a Petition for Dismissal, a Certificate of Rehabilitation, or sealing an arrest record, we provide accessible, affordable, and compassionate legal support.

We proudly serve clients across California—including Alameda County, San Francisco County, San Mateo County, Los Angeles County, Hayward, Sacramento, Riverside, San Diego, Santa Clara, and beyond.
Our Values
Dignity Through Justice
We believe every Californian deserves the right to pursue a better future after fulfilling their legal obligations.
Legal Access for All
Whether you live in San Jose or Hayward, we’re committed to making legal remedies like expungement and record sealing affordable and attainable.
Second Chances Matter
Your past shouldn’t hold your future hostage. We provide legal pathways to employment, housing, and freedom from stigma.
Empowerment Through Law
From the moment you contact us to the final court order, our goal is to make you feel seen, heard, and supported.
